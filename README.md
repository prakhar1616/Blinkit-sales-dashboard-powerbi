# 🛒 Blinkit Sales Dashboard 📊
An interactive sales analysis dashboard built using Power BI to analyze the performance of Blinkit – India’s Last Minute App.
This dashboard provides deep insights into total sales, outlet performance, item distribution, customer ratings, and sales trends over the years.

# 📌 Project Overview
The goal of this project is to analyze Blinkit's sales data and extract meaningful business insights using data visualization techniques. <br />
🔹 Total Sales: $1.20M <br />
🔹 Average Sales: $141 <br />
🔹 Number of Items: 8523 <br />
🔹 Average Rating: 3.9 ⭐ <br />
The dashboard helps in understanding: <br />
Sales trends 📈 <br />
Outlet performance 🏪 <br />
Item category contribution 🥦🥛 <br />
Customer satisfaction ⭐ <br />

# 🛠️ Tools & Technologies Used
📊 Power BI (Data Visualization & Dashboard Creation) <br />
🧮 DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) – For calculated measures & KPIs <br />
🔄 Power Query – For data cleaning & transformation <br />
📂 Data Modeling <br />
📈 Business Intelligence & Data Analysis <br />

# 📊 Key Dashboard Sections

## 1️⃣ Sales Overview
Total Sales, Average Sales, Number of Items, and Average Rating KPIs <br />
Year-wise sales trend visualization <br />
📌 Insight: <br />
Sales peaked in 2018 ($205K) and showed a drop in 2020, followed by recovery. <br />

## 2️⃣ Outlet Establishment Trend 🏗️
Displays total sales by establishment year. <br />
📌 Insight: <br />
Major growth observed after 2012. <br />
Strongest sales recorded in 2018. <br />
Slight decline after 2019 but stable performance maintained. <br />

## 3️⃣ Outlet Size Analysis 🏬
Medium outlets generated the highest revenue (~$507.90K). <br />
Small and High-sized outlets followed behind. <br />
📌 Business Insight: <br />
Medium-sized outlets are the most profitable segment and contribute the highest share in total revenue. <br />

## 4️⃣ Outlet Location Analysis 📍
Tier 3 cities generated highest sales ($472.13K). <br />
Tier 2 cities performed moderately. <br />
Tier 1 cities generated comparatively lower sales. <br />
📌 Business Insight: <br />
Blinkit performs exceptionally well in Tier 3 cities, indicating strong demand in emerging markets. <br />

## 5️⃣ Item Type Performance 🛍️
Top-performing categories: <br />
🥦 Fruits & Vegetables ($0.18M) <br />
🍿 Snack Foods ($0.18M) <br />
🏠 Household Items ($0.14M) <br />

Lowest-performing categories: <br />
🐟 Seafood <br />
🥣 Breakfast <br />
📌 Business Insight: <br />
Daily essentials and snack items drive maximum revenue. <br />

## 6️⃣ Fat Content Analysis 🥛
Regular items contributed more revenue ($776K). <br />
Low-fat products contributed ($425K). <br />
📌 Business Insight: <br />
Customers prefer regular items over low-fat alternatives. <br />

# 📈 Overall Business Insights
✅ Medium-sized outlets are the most profitable. <br />
✅ Tier 3 cities drive the highest revenue. <br />
✅ Fruits, Snacks, and Household items are top revenue generators. <br />
✅ Sales peaked in 2018 and show stable long-term performance. <br />
✅ Customer rating average (3.9) indicates good satisfaction but scope for improvement. <br />

# 🚀 How This Dashboard Helps
Supports data-driven decision making 📊 <br />
Identifies high-performing outlet types 🏪 <br />
Helps optimize product categories 🛍️ <br />
Assists in expansion planning (focus on Tier 3 & medium outlets) 📍 <br />
